📚 About Rice Leaf Diseases

🔴 Bacterial Blight

Caused by Xanthomonas oryzae. Yellow, water-soaked lesions. Can reduce yield by up to 75%.

🟠 Blast

Caused by Magnaporthe oryzae. Diamond-shaped lesions. Most destructive rice disease worldwide.

🟤 Brown Spot

Caused by Bipolaris oryzae. Small dark brown lesions. Worsens with nutrient deficiency.

🟡 Tungro

Viral disease (RTBV + RTSV). Yellow-orange leaves, stunted growth. Spread by leafhoppers.
